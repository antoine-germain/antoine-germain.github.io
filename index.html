I am a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Pennsylvania School of Social Policy and Practice. I hold a PhD in Economics from CORE/LIDAM, UCLouvain obtained in June 2025 as part of the European doctoral program.

My research fields are public economics, labor economics and social choice theory. I evaluate public policies when agents have heterogeneous tastes and when labor markets are imperfect.
